# Statutory Safety Audit Report: LPG Storage Area Mounded Bullets

**Document ID:** AUD-DGFASLI-2025-S1
**Category:** Statutory Safety Audit Report
**Authority:** Directorate General Factory Advice Service and Labour Institutes (DGFASLI), India
**Asset Tag:** LPG-BULLET-101 (LPG Storage Facility)
**Plant:** IOCL Gujarat Refinery
**Reviewed By:** K. Nair (Lead Safety Auditor)
**Date:** April 15, 2025

---

## 1. Safety Audit Findings & Gaps
During the half-yearly statutory safety audit under the Manufacture, Storage and Import of Hazardous Chemical Rules, the mounded bullets safety instrumented systems were audited.
- **Safety Valve Testing Frequency:** OISD-STD-144 requires safety valves on mounded storage vessels to be tested and calibrated once every 12 months.
- **Audit Finding:** The safety relief valves (SRV-LPG-101A/B) calibration was last done on March 10, 2024.
- **Status:** Non-Compliant (Exceeded the 12-month statutory frequency by 35 days).

---

## 2. Risk Assessment
- **Hazard Class:** Major Accident Hazard (MAH)
- **Risk Level:** High (Potential overpressure event on LPG-BULLET-101).
- **Control Measure Needed:** Immediate shutdown of the bullet line loop to calibrate safety valves and verify interlock trip logic.

---

## 3. Remedial Action Plan
To close safety audit gap GAP-003:
- **Calibrate SRV-LPG-101A/B:** Scheduled for maintenance during next weekly sub-loop isolation on April 28, 2025.
- **Upgrade Interlock Logic:** Wire the high-pressure transmitter PT-LPG-101 to the CDU-1 ESD logic.
