# Environmental Compliance Record: FCC SOx Stack Emissions

**Document ID:** COMP-CPCB-2025-Q1
**Category:** Regulatory Compliance Record
**Authority:** Central Pollution Control Board (CPCB), India
**Asset Tag:** FCC-1 (Fluid Catalytic Cracker Unit)
**Plant:** IOCL Gujarat Refinery
**Reviewed By:** R. Sharma (Environmental Engineer)
**Date:** March 28, 2025

---

## 1. Compliance Gap & Environmental Limits
Under the Environment Protection Act (EP Act Schedule VI), the Central Pollution Control Board has updated SOx stack emission limits.
- **Old Stack Limit:** 100 mg/Nm³
- **Revised CPCB Limit:** 30 mg/Nm³ (Effective January 1, 2025)

---

## 2. Emission Monitoring Results
Continuous Stack Emission Monitoring Systems (CSEMS) recorded the following data for Q1 2025:
- **FCC-1 Stack SOx Average:** 42 mg/Nm³
- **Status:** Non-Compliant (Exceeds the revised limit by 12 mg/Nm³).

---

## 3. Corrective Action Plan
To resolve this compliance gap (GAP-002), the refinery is installing a Flue Gas Desulfurization (FGD) unit.
- **FGD Commissioning Due Date:** Sept 30, 2025.
- **Temporary Measure:** Injecting low-sulfur catalyst additives to reduce emissions.
