# OEM Specifications: E-101 Shell and Tube Heat Exchanger

**Document ID:** OEM-SPEC-E101
**Category:** Technical Specification Manual
**Asset Tag:** E-101 (CDU-1 Pre-heat Exchanger)
**Manufacturer:** Heavy Engineering Corp (HEC)
**Standards Compliance:** API 660, ASME Section VIII Div 1
**Design Type:** TEMA Type AES (Split Ring Floating Head)

---

## 1. Technical Data & Limits
- **Heat Duty:** 12.4 MW
- **Shell-side Medium:** Vacuum Residue
- **Tube-side Medium:** Crude Oil
- **Tube Wall Thickness (Nominal):** 2.77mm
- **Minimum Allowable Tube Wall (Safety Limit):** 2.0mm
- **Corrosion Allowance:** 1.0mm
- **Velocity Limit:** 4.2 m/s (tube inlet nozzle)

---

## 2. Materials of Construction
- **Shell Material:** Carbon Steel (CS)
- **Tube Material:** Carbon Steel (ASTM A179)
- **Upgrade Recommendation:** High-TAN crudes require upgrading tube bundles to SS316L (Stainless Steel) per API 660 recommendations.
