# Work Order Log: Heat Exchanger E-101 Tube Cleaning

**Document ID:** WO-2025-0142
**Category:** Maintenance Log Record
**Associated Asset:** E-101 (CDU-1 Exchanger)
**Job Description:** Tube bundle cleaning - hydrojetting
**Technician:** D. Singh
**Approved By:** K. Patel (Maintenance Supervisor)
**Completed Date:** January 8, 2025

---

## 1. Description of Work Done
Due to high pressure drop on shell side, exchanger E-101 was taken offline.
- Hydrojet cleaning performed on tube bundle at 10,000 PSI.
- Heavy scaling and hydrocarbon deposits removed.
- Visual inspection conducted post-cleaning.

---

## 2. Observations & Recommendations
Under-deposit corrosion was noted at the tube sheet and shell inlet nozzle area. Minimum wall thickness measured during UT survey was 2.1mm.

**Action Plan:** Tube bundle shows signs of accelerated corrosion. Replace the carbon steel bundle with SS316L during turnaround TA-2025.
