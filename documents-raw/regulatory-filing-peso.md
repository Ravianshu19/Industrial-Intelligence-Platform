## 1. Compliance Details
Under the Petroleum Act 1934 and Petroleum Rules 2002, storage tank TK-15 (Class A product) requires a valid PESO operating license.
- **License Number:** PESO/MH/REF-08/2020
- **Expiry Date:** April 15, 2025
- **Current Status:** EXPIRED

---

## 2. Compliance Gap Analysis
Due to the floating roof seal fire event (INC-2024-008) in August 2024, the mandatory post-fire safety inspection and tank bottom plate integrity survey were not signed off.
- **Statutory Gap:** Tank TK-15 cannot receive product under OISD-STD-116 and PESO guidelines until safety certification is submitted.

---

## 3. Corrective Steps
1. Complete the tank bottom plate MFL inspection during TA-2025.
2. File renewal form along with inspection report to PESO Nagpur head office.
