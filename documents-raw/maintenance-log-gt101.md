## 2. Safety Limits & ISO Compliance
Per the **ISO 10816-3** standard limits for rotary machines (Class III):
- **Normal Zone:** < 2.5 mm/s (Vibration levels are currently trending high)
- **Alert Zone:** 4.5 mm/s (Current reading of 2.8 mm/s is below alert threshold, but rising)
- **Trip Limit:** 7.5 mm/s
- **Bearing Temperature Limit:** Max allowable temperature is 95°C.

---

## 3. Analysis & AI Diagnostics
The 1X vibration level has grown from 1.8 mm/s to 2.8 mm/s over the last 6 months (a 56% increase). 
Vibration analysis matches the signature of inner race bearing wear. The temperature at bearing #1 is trending up to 82°C (historical norm is 70°C). 

**Recommendation:**
1. Spares spare parts (bearing kit Part-GT101-B1) lead time is 6 weeks. K. Patel must confirm order placement.
2. Schedule a bearing replacement during the upcoming May 2025 turnaround.
