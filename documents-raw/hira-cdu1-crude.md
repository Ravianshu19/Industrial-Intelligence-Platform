# Hazard Identification & Risk Assessment (HIRA): CDU-1 Crude Circuit

**Document ID:** HIRA-CDU1-CRUDE-2025
**Category:** Safety Risk Assessment
**Standard Reference:** ISO 45001 (OH&S Management Systems)
**Asset Tag:** E-101 (Heat Exchanger), PL-CDU1 (Piping Loop)
**Plant:** IOCL Gujarat Refinery
**Evaluated By:** K. Patel (Maintenance Supervisor)
**Date:** February 20, 2025

---

## 1. Process Hazards and Risks
The crude pre-heat circuit operates at elevated temperatures (350°C) and pressure (2.5 kg/cm²). The primary hazards identified are:
- **Hazard 1:** Flange leak leading to jet fire.
- **Hazard 2:** Thermal expansion leading to overpressure on shell-side.
- **Hazard 3:** Pipe wall thinning due to high-TAN crude corrosion.

---

## 2. Controls and Mitigations
- **Mitigation 1:** Grounding connections check to prevent static accumulation during high flow transfers.
- **Mitigation 2:** Annual pressure relief valve (PSV-101) calibration.
- **Mitigation 3:** Regular ultrasonic thickness (UT) survey on pipe rack PL-CDU1.
