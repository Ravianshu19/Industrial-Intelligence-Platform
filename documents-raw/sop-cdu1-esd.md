# SOP: Emergency Shutdown Procedure (ESD) for Crude Distillation Unit CDU-1

**Document ID:** SOP-CDU-1-ESD-04
**Category:** Operating Procedure (SOP)
**Plant:** IOCL Gujarat Refinery
**Associated Asset:** CDU-1, ESDV-101, T-101
**Author:** R. Sharma (Process Safety Lead)
**Status:** Active

---

## 1. Trigger Conditions for Emergency Shutdown
The CDU-1 Emergency Shutdown must be manually initiated or will automatically trigger under the following safety instrumented system (SIS) conditions:
- **Condition A:** Tube inlet temperature at Heat Exchanger E-101 exceeds 375°C.
- **Condition B:** Reactor outlet pressure exceeds critical limit.
- **Condition C:** Hydrocarbon gas leak concentration exceeds 35% LEL at HCUreactor piping flange.

---

## 2. Sequence of Safety Actions
Once triggered, the ESD logic executes the following steps:
1. **ESDV-101 Isolation:** The main feed isolation valve ESDV-101 must close within 15 seconds.
2. **Fractionator T-101 Steam Cutoff:** Stripping steam valves to column T-101 are automatically isolated.
3. **Depressurization:** Divert process vapor stream to the refinery Flare System (Flare-1) via Knockout Drum (KO-Drum-1).
4. **Water Deluge:** If flame is detected in the area, activate fire water deluge system FF-System-2.
